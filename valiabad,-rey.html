Valiabad, Rey

Valiabad (Persian: ولي آباد, also Romanized as Valīābād; also known as Valīābād-e Beheshtī and Vallad-e Beheshtī) is a village in Azimiyeh Rural District, in the Central District of Ray County, Tehran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 3,252, in 918 families.
