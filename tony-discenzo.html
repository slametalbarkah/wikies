Tony Discenzo

Anthony N. Discenzo (February 4, 1936 – February 11, 2007) was an American football tackle who played one season in the American Football League (AFL) with the Boston Patriots and Buffalo Bills. He played college football at Michigan State University and attended Cathedral Latin High School in Cleveland, Ohio.
