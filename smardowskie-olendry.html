Smardowskie Olendry

Smardowskie Olendry [smarˈdɔfskʲɛ ɔˈlɛndrɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Ostrów Wielkopolski, within Ostrów Wielkopolski County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland.
