Maksym Sasovskyi

Maksym Serhiyovych Sasovskyi (Ukrainian: Максим Сергійович Сасовський; born 23 August 2001) is a Ukrainian professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for Ukrainian club Viktoriya Sumy.

Previously he played for Volyn Lutsk.
