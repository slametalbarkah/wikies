Pain Afrakoti

Pain Afrakoti (Persian: پايين افراكتي, also Romanized as Pā’īn Afrākotī; also known as Afrā Kotī and Pā’īn Afrā Kotī) is a village in Bisheh Sar Rural District, in the Central District of Qaem Shahr County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 592, in 168 families.
