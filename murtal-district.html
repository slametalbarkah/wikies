Murtal District

Bezirk Murtal (German: [ˈmuːʁˌtaːl, ˈmuɐ̯ˌtaːl]) is a district of the state of Styria in Austria. It was formed on January 1, 2012, through a merger of the former Judenburg District and Knittelfeld District.
