Zir-e Tut

Zir-e Tut (Persian: زيرتوت, also Romanized as Zīr-e Tūt) is a village in Chin Rural District, Ludab District, Boyer-Ahmad County, Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 174, in 38 families.
