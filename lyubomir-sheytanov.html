Lyubomir Sheytanov

Lyubomir Sheytanov (Bulgarian Cyrillic: Любомир Шейтанов) (born 17 July 1961 in Burgas) is a former Bulgarian footballer who played as a goalkeeper and currently the goalkeeping coach of Bulgaria.
