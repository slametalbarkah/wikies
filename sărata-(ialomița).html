Sărata (Ialomița)

The Sărata is a left tributary of the river Ialomița in Romania. It discharges into the Ialomița in Urziceni. It flows through the villages Sărata-Monteoru, Ulmeni, Movila Banului, Mihăilești, Glodeanu Sărat, Armășești, and Bărbulești. Its length is 72 km (45 mi) and its basin size is 1,290 km2 (500 sq mi).
