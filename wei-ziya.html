Wei Ziya

Wei Ziya (Chinese: 魏子雅; pinyin: Wèi Zǐyǎ; usually referred to in the media as Ziya Wei; born October 1984) is a Chinese model and beauty pageant titleholder who the winner of Miss Universe China 2007. Wei represented China in Miss Universe 2008 in Vietnam, but was unplaced.
