Mehdiabad-e Shur

Mehdiabad-e Shur (Persian: مهدي ابادشور, also Romanized as Mehdīābād-e Shūr; also known as Mehdīābād, Mehdīābād Āb Shūr and Mehdi Abad Hoomeh Zarand) is a village in Mohammadabad Rural District, in the Central District of Zarand County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 88, in 21 families.
