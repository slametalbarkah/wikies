Mykhaylo Kaluhin

Mykhaylo Kaluhin (Ukrainian: Михайло Петрович Калугін; born 20 November 1994) is a Ukrainian professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for Kyrgyz Premier League club Abdysh-Ata.





== Career ==

In February 2016, Kaluhin signed a two-year contract with Belarusian Premier League club Krumkachy Minsk.

On 4 March 2025, Kaluhin was announced as part of Abdysh-Ata's squad for the 2025 season.
