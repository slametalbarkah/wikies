Martina Berta

Martina Berta (born 25 March 1998) is an Italian cross-country mountain biker. She is the bronze medalist of 2024 World mountain bike championships in Cross-country Olympic.

She competed in the women's cross-country event at the 2024 Summer Olympics, placing 14th. She is also a five-time elite national champion.
