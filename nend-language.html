Nend language

Nend (Nent), or Angaua, is a Papuan language spoken by the Angaua people of Madang Province, Papua New Guinea. It is spoken in Pasinkap village (5.021378°S 144.841326°E﻿ / -5.021378; 144.841326﻿ (Pasinkap)) of Arabaka Rural LLG, Madang Province.
